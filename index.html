FAQ

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Do you help with insurance claims?

Yes — storm and hail damage claims are a major part of our business. We provide a free roof inspection, document all damage with photos, and work directly with your insurance adjuster. Our team has handled hundreds of claims across the Waco area.

What types of roofing do you handle?

Everything — residential shingles, commercial flat roofs (TPO, EPDM, PVC), metal roofing, steel buildings, barndominiums, silicone coatings, ACM panels, and historic restoration. If it has a roof, we can handle it.

Do you offer free estimates?

Yes. Every estimate starts with a free roof inspection — no obligation. We'll give you an honest assessment of what your roof needs and a clear quote before any work begins.

Are you available for emergencies?

Absolutely. Our 24-hour emergency line is (254) 255-1518. When severe weather hits, we can tarp and secure your roof to prevent further damage until a full repair or replacement can be scheduled.

How long has Clark Roofing been in business?

Since 1987 — nearly four decades serving Waco and Central Texas. We're BBB Accredited with an A+ rating and hold Texas roofing license ROOF9863795. We've outlasted the storm chasers because we do quality work and stand behind it.
